About Us

John Kenneth B Bartolome, a businessman, founded the Kenneth's Bartolome Salon in 2023. With the help of his co-partners Keanna and Kristine, the trio quickly grew the business to what it is today. The award-winning business partners have knuckled down and continue to work tirelessly to improve and maintain business benchmarks, and are now ecstatic with the unveiling of the new Beauty and Hair in Alabang, Muntinlupa City. "Because our customers are so important to us, we train our specialists on a regular basis to ensure that all medicines are of the highest quality and that every customer receives the best care."
